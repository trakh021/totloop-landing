YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before joining the loop.
How does TotLoop verify listings?
Every listing is manually reviewed against the ACCC product safety recalls register before it goes live. Sellers must declare the item's condition, age, and any known safety history. Items flagged on the recall register are removed immediately and the seller is notified.
What items can I list on TotLoop?
Prams and strollers, car seats (under 10 years old), cots and nursery furniture, baby carriers, clothing 0–8 years, and toys. Items currently on the ACCC recall register or prohibited under Australian Consumer Law cannot be listed. Full prohibited items list available in our safety policy.
Is my payment protected if something goes wrong?
Yes. All payments go through secure escrow, held until the buyer confirms the item was received as described. If there's a dispute, our team steps in to resolve it. Buyers are never left exposed on TotLoop.
When is TotLoop launching?
We're currently building our founding member community ahead of launch. The first 500 sellers join completely free. Everyone on the waitlist will be personally notified before we go live — you won't miss it.
What does it cost to sell on TotLoop?
Founding members — the first 500 sellers — pay zero commission forever. No subscription, no per-listing fees, no hidden charges. Standard member pricing will be announced at launch. Founding spots are limited and filling fast.
What about local pickup — how does escrow work?
For local pickups, TotLoop holds payment in escrow until the buyer confirms the item is as described after inspecting it in person. This protects both parties without requiring shipping — and eliminates cash transactions with unverified strangers.
